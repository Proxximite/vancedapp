Install

Non-Root Root Magisk
Root
Step One

Uninstall all YouTube updates and disable auto-update in the Play Store

Step Two

Download the installer

Step Three

Download a suitable root APK for your device.

Step Four

Place the downloaded root apk into /sdcard/downloads or inside the installation ZIP

Step Five

Flash the installer ZIP in TWRP

Step Six

Reboot your device and you should see Vanced in your app drawer. If you do not see Vanced verify that all updates have been removed, or disable YouTube

Step Seven

Enjoy!
